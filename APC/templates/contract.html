System of contract

Article 1 service content
1. The services referred to in this agreement include: Party B provides online trading platform for Party A to release information and materials related to the project auction,and other related services provided by Party B to Party A through online trading platform for online auction trading activities. 2. The online trading platform provided is not an "auction" as stipulated in the auction law of the people's Republic of China and relevant laws and regulations, but a simple "bidding" mode. Party B does not participate in the online auction itself as a buyer or auctioneer.
Article 2 service charge rules
1. Service charge standard Estimated service fee = final auction amount of each auction item × 5% Refund amount = starting price of goods without auction × 10% 2. Refund method Withdraw the balance in the user's account to the bank card bound by the user.
